### **2. DECLARATION OF INTENT & ALIGNMENT**

On behalf of **[INSERT NAME OF HEALTHCARE / RESEARCH INSTITUTION]** (hereafter referred to as the *"Clinical Partner"*), we hereby declare our strong interest and intent to participate as a **Clinical Validation and Research Partner** in the development, testing, and deployment of the **AETERNA Virtual Human Twin (VHT)** platform.

As an institution committed to cutting-edge oncology treatment and clinical innovation, we recognize that standard-of-care (SOC) methods for aggressive, mutation-shielded tumors often run into therapeutic limits. The biophysical in-silico modeling approach offered by AETERNA-VHT, operating at Technology Readiness Level 6 (TRL 6) with high-speed deterministic HL7/FHIR ingress, represents a significant leap forward in reducing off-target toxicities and predicting patient-specific combination sweeps.

---

### **3. SCOPE OF COLLABORATION**

Subject to standard institutional approvals, local ethical committee reviews, and formal consortium agreements, the Clinical Partner intends to collaborate with AETERNA Sovereign Labs across the following defined action areas:

#### **A. Clinical Ingress & HL7/FHIR Protocol Validation**
*   Provide clinical and technical feedback on the **Cognitive Ingress Alignment Layer (CIAL)** to ensure accurate mapping of unstructured genomic narratives into standardized LOINC codes:
    *   `TP53` Mutation Status [LOINC: `85337-4`]
    *   `KRAS` Mutation Status [LOINC: `62358-7`]
    *   `EGFR` Mutation Status [LOINC: `62357-9`]
    *   `PD-L1` TPS Expression [LOINC: `85147-7`]
*   Ensure that the Ingress daemons integrate smoothly with active Hospital Information Systems (HIS) and PACS databases via secure, local FHIR APIs.

#### **B. Retrospective Cohort Validation & GDPR Compliance**
*   Support the expansion of the AETERNA-VHT biophysical reference database by executing localized, retrospective cohort evaluations on historic patient data.
*   All data exchanges and local computation loops will be executed under **100% GDPR-compliant, zero-trust terms**, leveraging AETERNA’s secure on-premise execution nodes (AMD Ryzen/NVIDIA H100 clusters) without transferring patient identifiable information outside the hospital intranet.

#### **C. Research Use Only (RUO) Telemetry HUD Deployment**
*   Deploy and test the interactive **AETERNA VHT Suite HUD** (`vht_suite.html` / `hud.html`) within academic and research departments under **Research Use Only (RUO)** licensing terms.
*   Enable clinical teams and bio-engineers to run parallel mock therapeutic sweeps side-by-side with active clinical trials to evaluate the platform’s Concordance Index ($C$-index) and target selection precision.

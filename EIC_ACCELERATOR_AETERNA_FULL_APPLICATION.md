## SECTION 1: EXCELLENCE

### 1.1 Quality and Pertinence of the Project's R&I Content

#### The Problem: Europe's Critical Software Security Gap
Europe's digital infrastructure faces an existential convergence of threats:
1. **The Cost Barrier**: 80% of European SMEs have never performed a professional security audit due to prohibitive costs ($5,000–$50,000).
2. **Workforce Shortage**: A global deficit of 4 million cybersecurity professionals makes human-dependent security unsustainable.
3. **Quantum Threat**: NIST finalized ML-KEM and ML-DSA standards in 2024, yet <2% of European enterprises have begun PQC migration.
4. **AI-Generated Attacks**: Polymorphic malware and industrial-scale phishing are outpacing traditional signature-based defenses.
5. **Sovereignty Deficit**: Total reliance on US-based tools (Qualys, Tenable) creates data residency and transparency risks.

#### The Solution: Project AETERNA
AETERNA is a production-ready, AI-powered autonomous cybersecurity platform that scans, detects, and self-heals digital infrastructure vulnerabilities entirely offline, ensuring 100% data sovereignty.
